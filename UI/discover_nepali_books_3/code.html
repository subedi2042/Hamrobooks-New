About the Book

Set in Kathmandu, Nepal, during the mid-20th century, Siris Ko Phul (The Blue Mimosa) is a masterpiece of modern Nepali literature. It explores the themes of existentialism, unrequited love, and the fragility of human existence through the perspective of a middle-aged veteran.
